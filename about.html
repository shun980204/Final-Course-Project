20 Years Experience

We are a family company dedicated to integral cleaning and maintenance services with 20 years of experience.

CLEANX has a structure superior to the usual in the cleaning sector, which brings great advantages to our customers: we make instead all the investment in machinery, tools, training, organization and management essential to provide a highly effective service.

Our company was created in response to the growing needs of a demanding market that increasingly demanded a more personalized attention and a higher quality service.

CLEANX has an excellent team whose main objective is to achieve customer satisfaction. Since our beginnings we have focused on training a staff of efficient and skilled professionals to provide answers and solutions tailored to the particularities of each client entity.
