1 How much time on average do you need to do a spot cleaning of a house, office or business?
We usually do it in the course of a morning or an afternoon depending on the tasks to be performed and the size of the property, as we move with a cleaning team consisting of as many people as the size and distribution of the surface to be cleaned allows us. In some cases, with a property larger than 250 meters, we can take 12 hours to perform the cleaning.
2 What is the procedure for hiring an a deep cleaning service, general cleaning, etc.?
After contacting us via email, phone or visits to our offices, we agree a previous visit to your home, office, premises at the time that best suits you to assess everything you want to do and thus, in less than 24 hours to offer you the best possible budget without any commitment. From the acceptance, we specify the date and time that best suits you to perform the cleaning service.
3 What cleaning products and machines are used and are they included in the price?
The cleaning products we use are industrial products adapted to each type of cleaning, with a reduced aggressiveness towards the materials, being neutral detergents, liquids with self-sliding, anti-dust and anti-static treatment. We avoid using acid or abrasive materials.
4 Do I need to be in the property while you perform the cleaning service?
In our customized cleanings, we ask as essential that the client is present to supervise the work of the team during the entire cleaning. We understand that each client may have a personalized service, and what better way than to distribute the team according to their needs or changes that may occur during the course of the contracted cleaning. With over 18 years of experience, we believe that our team of professionals, so that their work can be recognized, and the customer is satisfied with an optimal work, should be directed throughout the service so that, in the event that something is not to your liking, we can provide a solution.
5 How is the payment for services made?
In the case of customized cleanings, the payment will be 100% in its totality when hiring the service, having 24-48 hrs to carry it out. Once the payment is made, we will proceed to block the cleaning equipment for the day you request. To make the payment we adapt to the form that suits you, being able to be in cash at our office or by bank transfer. In home services, the payment is by direct debit at the end of each month, once all the services have been performed, and it can also be in cash at our office.
